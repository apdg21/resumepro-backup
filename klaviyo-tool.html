Klaviyo Analytics Dashboard

Upload your Klaviyo CSV export to visualize campaign performance, trends, growth, and seasonal patterns.

How to Use

Follow these steps to analyze your Klaviyo campaigns:

1Export CSV from Klaviyo
  • Go to Analytics → Campaigns
  • Select your date range, click Export → CSV
  • Ensure columns include: Campaign Name, Send Time, Subject, Total Recipients, Unique Opens, Unique Clicks, Revenue, Open Rate, Click Rate, Placed Order Rate, Send Weekday, List
2Download sample CSV (optional)

Test the dashboard with sample data first:

Download Sample CSV
3Upload & filter

Click Choose file, select your CSV, optionally set a date range, then click Analyse. All tabs will populate automatically.

4Explore your data

Browse Daily, Weekly, Monthly, Trends, Growth, Seasonal, and Key Insights tabs. Export any view as CSV.
