How to Delete Your Data in Notes & Reminders

All your data (reminders and voice notes) is stored locally on your device only. We do not store, transmit, or retain any data on our servers.

To delete individual reminders or voice notes:

  1. Open the Notes & Reminders app.
  2. Find the reminder or voice note you want to delete.
  3. Swipe left on the item.
  4. Tap the "Delete" button that appears.
  5. The item will be permanently removed from your device.

To delete ALL app data at once (nuclear option):

  1. Go to your device Settings → Apps → Notes & Reminders.
  2. Tap Storage.
  3. Tap Clear Data or Clear Storage.
  4. This will remove all reminders, voice notes, and app settings permanently.
